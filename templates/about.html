About Us

Who we are

Tundacom Kenya is an ecommerce platform owned by Four Space Real Estate. In addition to efficient property management, the company was started with the aim of aiding house search, house swap and flexible hotel booking.

Company mission statement

To be a regional leader in proving technological solutions to rental and accommodation businesses.

Company values and vision

Values: Honesty, integrity, fun and innovation.

Vision: To ease urban settlement and accommodation by solving customers’ challenges.

Tenants paying relatively the same rent can search and swap houses. For this to happen, the tenants must agree with their landlords on how to restore the houses or repair the houses themselves with the approval of the landlord. Any shortfall or excess arising in the deposits can then be topped up or refunded by the tenants to each other

This feature allows tenants looking for new houses to search and find their dream house within their desired locations.

In addition, one can list properties for sale for easy link with potential buyers.

What’s new to our hotel booking is that it allows for flexible hotel bookings which had not been offered in the market before. In addition to normal ways of booking where guests book full days, this feature allows them to book between 7 a.m. to 6 pm or 7p.m to 6 am.
